LUMIO Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Summary: LUMIO stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, share, or sell any personal information.

1. Data We Collect

LUMIO does NOT collect any personal data. All reminder data is stored locally on your device. We have no servers and no accounts.

2. Permissions We Use

  • Notifications: To send reminder alerts at scheduled times
  • Location (optional): Only for location-based reminders you set up. Never shared.
  • Microphone (optional): Only for voice input when you tap the mic button
  • Fingerprint (optional): Only for app lock if you enable it

3. Third-Party Services

  • OpenWeatherMap (optional): Used only if you add an API key. Your location is sent only when you request weather data.
  • Google Play Services: Used for location geofencing on Android devices

4. Data Storage

All reminders, categories, and settings are stored locally on your device only.
