LUMIO - Luminous Intelligent Organizer

Never forget anything important again! LUMIO is a beautiful, AI-powered reminder app that understands natural language and helps you stay organized every day.

KEY FEATURES:

AI CHAT ASSISTANT
Talk to LUMIO AI naturally: "Remind me every last Friday at 5 PM to submit the report" and it instantly creates the perfect reminder with the right category, priority and repeat schedule.

VOICE REMINDERS
Just speak your reminder out loud. LUMIO understands complex phrases like "Call mom tomorrow evening" and converts them to scheduled reminders automatically.

SMART LOCATION REMINDERS
Set reminders triggered by location. "Remind me to call manager when I reach office" - LUMIO monitors your location and fires the reminder at exactly the right moment.

WEATHER-BASED REMINDERS
Get smart alerts based on live weather. LUMIO notifies you to carry an umbrella when rain is expected, drink extra water during heat waves, or wear a jacket in cold weather.

HEALTH REMINDER TEMPLATES
15 pre-built health reminders for medicine, water, exercise, meditation, sleep and more. Add them all with one tap.

BEAUTIFUL DESIGN
Dark, Light, AMOLED and System themes. Gradient stat cards. Smooth animations. Looks great on any Android phone.

SMART CATEGORIES
10 built-in categories: Work, Home, Shopping, Bills, Study, Health, Events, Personal, Travel, Finance. AI auto-categorizes your reminders.

HOME SCREEN WIDGETS
3 widget styles: Small, Large, and Transparent. See your upcoming reminders without opening the app.

PRIVACY & SECURITY
PIN lock and fingerprint protection. Your data stays on your device. No account required.

STATISTICS & INSIGHTS
Track your completion rate, productive hours, streak days and more.

MULTILINGUAL
Full support for English, Malayalam, Hindi, Tamil and Telugu.

RECURRING REMINDERS
Daily, weekly, monthly, yearly or custom repeat schedules. Never miss important recurring tasks.

PRIORITY LEVELS
Mark reminders as Urgent, High, Medium, Low or None. Color-coded for quick scanning.

MOTIVATIONAL QUOTES
Daily quote to keep you inspired and productive.

BACKUP & RESTORE
Export and import your reminders. Never lose your data.

NOTIFICATION ACTIONS
Snooze for 5, 15 or 30 minutes or mark as done directly from the notification.

OPEN SOURCE
LUMIO is built with modern Android development: Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Room, Hilt, WorkManager.
